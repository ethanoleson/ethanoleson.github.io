Nearly 900 symbols with cartographic specifications for geologic maps are provided with U.S.Geological Survey Open-file Report 95-526.  The symbols were prepared for U.S. GeologicalSurvey Open-file Report 95-525, "Cartographic and Digital Standard for Geologic MapInformation."  Symbols in the "eps" files should be used in conjunction with the printeddraft standard for it explains the application and proper use of the symbols as well as the content and the color and pattern standards for geologic maps.  The symbols can be imported intoprograms, such as, but not limited to, Adobe Illustrator, Corel Draw, and AutoCad.  The original release is on two DOS diskettes.
